About The Essay Bros

We built a course that combines elite admissions strategy with real accountability.

Our Story

The Essay Bros started as a small group of mentors helping friends get into their dream schools. Today we run a structured, skills-first course that walks students from blank page to final submission — with feedback, templates, and a system that works.

Our team has attended UPenn Wharton, Johns Hopkins, Stanford, UCLA, UC Berkeley, Brown, Duke, and more. We bring the insights of competitive admissions rooms and turn them into a clear weekly playbook for students who want results.
