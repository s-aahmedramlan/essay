Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Essay Bros course experience

How does the course work? +

You work through weekly modules with clear deliverables and templates. Each week includes lessons, a draft sprint, and mentor feedback so you always know the next step.

Will my essays be original and authentic? +

Absolutely. We never write your essays for you. We provide prompts, feedback, and structure so your voice stays authentic and original.

How long does the process take? +

The core course runs 6 weeks, but you can move faster if you start early. Each module is designed for a one-week sprint.

Who gives feedback? +

Mentors from top universities review your drafts. Elite Mentorship students also receive 1:1 sessions.

What if I'm not satisfied with my draft? +

We include revision checkpoints in every module so you can improve quickly. If you need extra feedback, you can book a mentor review.

Do you offer payment plans? +

Yes. We can split Core Cohort and Elite Mentorship into 2-3 payments. Reach out after applying.

Can you help with waitlist letters (LOCIs)? +

Yes. We include a dedicated LOCI guide and examples. Learn more about LOCIs here.

What about appeal letters? +

We provide an appeal guide and checklist when it makes sense. Learn more about appeals here.

Is my information kept confidential? +

Yes. Course access is protected by verified logins and we never share student data.

What makes you different from other essay services? +

We teach a system that works under real deadlines: templates, structured sprints, and mentor feedback from graduates of top programs.
